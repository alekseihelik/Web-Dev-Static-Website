Some Nice Spots!

Prospect Park

A really nice place to visit!
It has a zoo and museums if you want to visit those, but it's also a nice place to just walk around.
It's the second largest park in Brooklyn, only 4 acres behind Marine Park.

Calvert Vaux Park

A relatively smaller park, but also still nice to walk around.
It's also near water, which is nice.
There's some soccer fields that you can play in whenever you want.
